About

I'm a Senior Applied Scientist at Salesforce AI Research in the Bay Area, where I sit at the intersection of research and product. My work spans model training, agent architectures, benchmarking, evaluation, and everything in between.

My long-term goal is to build systems that elevate human capability — technologies that sharpen judgment, expand creativity, and help individuals and institutions become more intentional, effective, and principled in how they operate.

Before Salesforce, I built conversational AI at Embodied Inc., did research with Prof. Julian McAuley at UC San Diego, and interned at Q-Centrix building ML models for healthcare. I hold an MS in CS (AI) from UCSD and a BE from R.V. College of Engineering, Bangalore.

Outside of work, I enjoy travelling and extreme sports.
